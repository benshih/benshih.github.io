Lose Yourself - Virtual Reality Environment

In this project, we brainstormed a design for a virtual reality environment and analyzed what aspects of it were feasible. We examined current technologies and made predictions about the future of virtual reality.

This project was undertaken as part of Montgomery Blair High School's Exploravision Project in spring 2008.
