Pinhole Camera

In this project, we applied our knowledge of optics and light to building a pinhole camera. We also practiced developing our own photos. The black and white images are due to the film that we used - color images were possible with this camera but the film would have been more expensive.

This project was undertaken as part of Montgomery Blair High School's Optics course in spring 2009.
