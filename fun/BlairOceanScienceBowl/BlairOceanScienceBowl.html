Ocean Science Bowl

To put some of our ocean science knowledge to use, we participated in the Ocean Science Bowl. Each round of the competition is a science trivia battle between two teams, and typically the winner is the team can answer the most questions correctly and quickly. Teams are penalized for incorrect answers, discouraging attempts to guess arbitrarily.

This activity was undertaken as part of Montgomery Blair High School's Ocean Science Bowl team in spring 2009.
